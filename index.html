What MAPPHEX Does

It turns business registration into an active operations workspace.

A business registers, selects the service it needs, accepts the subscription terms, and opens the workspace tools required for staff, records, sales, stock, finance, reporting, and support.

Creates organization workspaces

Each business gets its own workspace with administrator access, staff accounts, service modules, branch records, and subscription controls.

Connects services to daily work

Selected services open the tools needed for sales, inventory, finance, HR, departments, hospitality, health, retail, products, services, reports, and support.

Controls users, branches, and reports

Managers can add staff, assign permissions, separate branches, review activity, track records, and upgrade the subscription as the business grows.
