Price Components

What each subscription pays for.

The final subscription depends on the selected plan, number of users, enabled modules, branches, and support level.

Base platform feeCovers the organization workspace, secure access, hosting structure, and core account management.
ModulesOrganizations subscribe to the tools they need, such as sales, finance, HR, operations, inventory, or administration.
Users and staffPlans include a staff limit, and larger teams can move to a higher plan or request custom capacity.
BranchesBusinesses with multiple locations can subscribe to branch support for separate teams, records, and reports.
Setup supportOnboarding support helps configure the organization profile, modules, departments, roles, and initial workspace structure.
Renewal periodSubscriptions can be handled monthly for flexibility or yearly for a longer operating period.
How To Subscribe

Simple subscription flow for an organization or business.

A business starts by choosing a plan, then completes registration and activates the tools required for daily operations.

1. Choose a planSelect Starter, Business, or Enterprise based on company size and operational needs.
2. Register organizationSubmit the business name, contact details, organization email, and administrator account.
3. Select modulesChoose the departments and tools the organization needs before opening the workspace.
4. Confirm subscriptionReview the plan, included limits, billing period, and any setup support required.
5. Activate workspaceAfter confirmation, the organization workspace becomes ready for users, branches, and records.
6. Renew or upgradeThe organization can renew, upgrade, add modules, or request support as operations grow.
How Subscription Works

Subscriptions stay connected to the organization workspace.

Each subscription belongs to one registered organization and controls the modules, staff capacity, branches, and support level available in that workspace.

Organization-based

Every business subscribes under its own organization profile, so access and billing remain separate.

Module-based

The organization activates only the tools it needs, which keeps the workspace focused and easier to manage.

Renewable

Subscriptions can be renewed monthly or yearly, depending on the preferred billing arrangement.

Upgradeable

A business can upgrade when it adds more staff, branches, departments, records, or support requirements.
