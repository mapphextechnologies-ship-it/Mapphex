Data Protection Policies

Policies for safer organization data handling.

These policies guide how MAPPHEX should protect organization records, staff access, documents, operational activity, and account information across every workspace.

Data minimization

Collect only the information needed to register, verify, support, and operate each organization workspace.

Permission control

Give users access only to the modules, branches, departments, and records required for their role.

Organization isolation

Keep each organization workspace separated so records from one business are not exposed to another.

Account protection

Protect login flows, session access, organization IDs, staff accounts, and sensitive profile details.

Audit accountability

Record key changes so administrators can review updates, user activity, access changes, and module events.

Secure documents

Handle uploaded or generated documents with controlled access, clear ownership, and careful retention.

Retention discipline

Keep operational data only as long as it is needed for service delivery, reporting, compliance, or support.

Incident response

Respond to suspicious access, account issues, data errors, and service disruptions with documented action.
