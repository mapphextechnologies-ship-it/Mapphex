Core Capabilities

Everything starts from the organization workspace.

Each registered business gets a workspace that can support teams, branches, departments, modules, subscriptions, reporting, and access control.

01

Organization profiles

Store business details, contact information, organization identity, workspace settings, and subscription status.

02

Staff management

Add staff accounts, assign responsibilities, organize departments, and control access to workspace tools.

03

Branch operations

Support multiple locations with branch-specific records, staff, operations, and reporting views.

04

Module activation

Enable the tools that fit the organization, including finance, sales, HR, operations, and administration.
